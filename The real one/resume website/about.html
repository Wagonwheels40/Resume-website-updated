I have previously worked in several different industries. My most recent role has been at ‘Statewide Independent Wholesalers’ in Tasmania, which is a distribution warehouse where I worked for 2 years. I have gone into more detail on the tab 'Work & Study timeline', but I thought I’d briefly talk about what led me to my interest in coding.

I enjoyed working at Statewide Independent Wholesalers, but it was a very physically demanding job which I couldn't see myself doing on the long term. It became clear to me that I had to look for something more rewarding, and this is when I decided to enrol for a Certificate III of IT at TAFE. It was the first IT course I studied, but I quickly became invested to the programming cluster of the course. I would spend hours a day working through the modules on ‘freecodecamp’ and ‘sololearn’ to improve my skills, and then I started working on projects I found on 'Github'. Through this course, I learned how to make basic websites and learn the fundamentals of my first programming language, 'Python'.

I then decided I wanted to pursue a career as a front-end software developer, as I enjoyed the design aspect of building websites through 'HTML' and 'CSS'. I also have a goal of creating apps for Android, which is why I've been self studying 'Javascript' and 'C#' as well. I have made a 'Github' for all of my projects I've been working on and I plan to expand this page as I learn more everyday. I have been learning coding for approximately 6 months now, and I’m still as eager to working on small projects and coding as I was when I first started my IT course.

The 'Coding Work' tab will give you an indication of my current proficiency. I hope to be given an opportunity to showcase my skills and help out in a team environment.
