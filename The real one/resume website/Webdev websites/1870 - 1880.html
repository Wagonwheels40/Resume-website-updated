The German Empire

For years, Germany was made up of various states which were independent from one another. However, in 1867, Bismarck decided to unify some of the northern states in Germany under the power of Prussia. Subsequently, in 1870-1871, Prussia defeated the French in the Franco-Prussian war. This defeat marked the foundation of the German Empire.

Austria-Hungary

The Austrian Hapsburgs formed a dual monarchy with Hungary in what is often referred to as the Austro-Hungarian Empire. This was an allegiance which was created by the emperor and king of Hungary, Franz Joseph.
